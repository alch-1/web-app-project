How it works

Step 1

Sign up with your Google account

Registration is as easy as connecting your Google account and filling in a few details about what you would use our site for. After a quick 5 minute registration, you're all set to start using Gig-E!

Step 2

Create or search for a gig

If you are a business owner looking for someone to fill your establishment with music, look no further. Our prebuilt forms will help you get your request to musicians with ease. And for those budding musicians looking for a place to show off their talent, Gig-E is the optimal place to start looking for that perfect gig.

Step 3

Get connected to clients with ease

Once you post a gig, simply wait for applicants to apply for it. Your dashboard will show all the applicants that have applied thus far, where you can take your pick by looking through their profiles and finding the musician that suits your needs. Musicians should strive to develop an interesting profile to increase their chances of being selected for a gig.

Why choose Gig-E?

Gig-E is made by Singaporeans, for Singaporeans. By using Gig-E, you will be able to access a whole host of local talent. Furthermore, Gig-E is more than just a freelancing site, as you can build and develop your profile with us, allowing you to have a greater presence and find gigs with ease. Lastly, Gig-E is entirely free to use, so theres no need to worry about monthly subscriptions or hidden costs. Simply find a gig and start jamming!
